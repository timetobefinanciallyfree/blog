Best Books on Investing for Beginners

Learn how to invest your money

Posted by Danny on February 16, 2022

Investing can be a daunting subject for a lot of people. A lot of people are afraid to jump in as they are afraid of losing money by making the wrong investment, trusting the wrong sources, or feeling like they don't have enough money to start investing.

Whether you are a new investor who doesn't know the best strategy for your portfolio or you haven't started investing yet. The books I have recommended below will cover all different aspects of the investments for beginners. I have provided links to The Book Depository as they have free delivery worldwide and a wide selection of books.

1. The Little Book That Still Beats the Market

This book was originally published in 2005, but it is considered one of the classics in finance literature. This updated version explains the basic principles of investing in the stock market successfully using the author's time-tested formula for buying stocks when they hit their bottom prices. The author explains all of these concepts in easy to read language without jargon. Perfect for a beginner. The book also includes how the author's formula performed during the financial crisis.

2. A Random Walk Down Wall Street

This best selling book provides an explanation of the main types of investing, from stocks, bonds, tangible assets like gold and collectibles to real estate investment trusts. It is a guide to investing and different strategies. The updated version of the book also has a chapter on how our emotions affect our investment strategies.

3. The Intelligent Investor

This is a classic in financial literature. The book was first published in 1949 and has remained one of the most respected guides to investing. This book will help you develop long-term strategies, avoid making losses, and how to avoid making emotional investing decisions. This is a must-read for any beginner investor.
